5.13 Interactive checking of symbol contents

As described in Searching for specific text, you can use Attributes → Find Text/Check Symbol... to look up for specific text. It opens the text search widget at the bottom of the window. If you click on the option button at the left hand side of the widget and select the option ‘Check Symbol:’, you’ll be able to check the symbol page you work with almost the same way as you would do it with lepton-symcheck though in the interactive mode. Choosing the option hides widget’s text entry and only two buttons, ‘Find’ and ‘Cancel’, are available then. Clicking on ‘Cancel’ closes the widget. Hit the ‘Find’ button and lepton-schematic will check your page and open a dialog window with results of the check. After looking at the issues found and hitting ‘OK’, a new window will be opened where your can select and zoom on any problematic object on the page.
