1 Overview

Lepton EDA suite is a set of tools that provide schematic capture, netlisting and export of schematics into several formats, which, in turn, allows simulation via third party tools and producing PCBs.

The project is over twenty years old, and has matured to the point where our design tools are suitable for use by students, professionals, educators, hobbyists, consultants, and anybody who needs a set of quality EDA tools.
