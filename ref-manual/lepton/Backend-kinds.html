6.3.2 Backend kinds

lepton-netlist backends are different by their destination. Currently, there are following kinds of backends in Lepton:

  • Backends for creating BOM (Bill Of Materials) or list of parts.
  • Backends for DRC (Design Rule Checking).
  • Backends for simulation.
  • Backends for PCB creation.
  • Backends for automation.
  • Backends for testing contents of a schematic.
