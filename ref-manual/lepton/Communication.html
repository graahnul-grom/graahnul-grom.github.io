11 Communication

We are using the following resources for almost all communication about Lepton EDA and gEDA:

  • our site on Github: report and discuss issues, add your patches, join to the project
  • gitter.im chat room: get in touch with Lepton EDA users and developers
  • geda-user mailing list: discuss anything about Lepton and gEDA

Do not hesitate to get in touch the current maintainers privately. We very appreciate any contribution and would be glad to help you if you have any trouble with Lepton.

We are interested in having new developers join and stay with our project. Get involved!
