8.5 Credits and history of lepton-attrib

The original code of lepton-attrib under name gattrib has been written by Stuart Brorson (sdb@cloud9.net) for the gEDA project. gattrib was cobbled together in December 2003 from parts culled from GtkSheet (GTK+Extra) and gEDA.
